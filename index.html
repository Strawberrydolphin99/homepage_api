Alina Pinardi is an architect and designer with a Master's degree in Architecture from ETH Zurich. Her practice maintains a strong focus on process, developing projects from concept to building. She explores how materials, form, and function interact, balancing precision and research with conceptual experimentation. She investigates the tension between functionality, atmosphere, and perception to produce work that is formally refined, spatially sensitive, and emotionally resonant.

alinapinardi is an architectural practice working across space, material, and process. The work spans building, adaptive transformations, and spatial installations, with a focus on how environments influence perception, behavior, and emotional experience. Projects are developed through research and material investigation, balancing conceptual clarity with sensory depth.


Alina Pinardi is an architect and designer holding a Master's degree in Architecture from ETH Zurich. She works across design and all phases of the building process, from concept to realization.
